pm5 video overlay

Record a video of a Concept2 Ergometer session overlaid with pace information.

Download video

Use cases

  • Coach wants to provide technique video and showing pace / stroke / distance / time information.
  • Athlete wants to compare old and recent technique sessions simultaneously with pace /stroke / distance / time information.

Requires

  • Chrome web browser
  • Laptop / computer with a camera and Bluetooth (not mobile compatible)
  • PM5-enabled ergometer
  • Optional: video editing tool of your choice

Usage

  • Point camera at rower / SkiErg / BikeErg.
  • Enable Bluetooth (More Options > Turn Wireless On) on PM5.
  • Click Connect to PM5 above. Wait until connection is complete. This takes a few seconds; be patient.
  • When ready, click Start recording.
  • When you're done, click Stop recording.
  • Download the video by clicking the Download video link.

FAQ

What is the output format for the video?
.webm
Does <my video editing software> work with .webm?
Possibly. There are plugins / importers and transcoders available; check the internet.
I don't see any video in the box above!
Make sure that you have given permission for this web page to access your camera. Don't worry, the video is not uploaded anywehere anywhere and stays only on this page until you decide to download it to your computer.
Does this thing send my video anywhere?
No. All processing occurs on this page in your browser on your computer. When you download the video, you only save it as a file on your computer. It goes nowhere else.
I don't have any specific video editing software. How do I view the video after I download it?
Drag the video file into an empty browser window and you'll see it.
